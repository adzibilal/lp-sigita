SIGITA ("SIGITA," "we," "us," or "our") has established this privacy statement ("Privacy Statement") to underline our dedication to maintaining your privacy when it comes to the information you provide to SIGITA through the App or Services (defined below). Ensuring privacy on SIGITA (the "App") is of utmost importance to us. As we gather important information from Users of the App and related services provided by SIGITA, including our mobile application (the "Services"), we've created this Privacy Statement to communicate our practices regarding information collection, use, and sharing. Collected Information
In general, the Personal Information we collect allows us to provide Services and gain insights into App and/or Services usage.
